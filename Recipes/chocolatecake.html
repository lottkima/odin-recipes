Easy Chocolate Cake

Whenever I get a sweet tooth, this is my go to dessert.

Whether you're a seasoned cook, or a true newb, you'll love this quick recipe.

    Ingredients

  • Boxed choclate cake
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/4c Oil
  • 1/2 Milk

    Directions

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Bake at 350 for 25 min
  3. poke with toothpic to check if its done
  4. Enjoy with ice cream
