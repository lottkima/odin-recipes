Farm Fresh Fried Eggs

Whenever my son wakes up, he always wants one thing for breakfast and one thing only...a fried egg.

Hey, look at me, I am a regular ol' blogger.

    Ingredients

  • an egg
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • a pan

    Directions

  1. Place butter in a pan and turn to med/high heat until it starts to sizzle
  2. When sizzling, crack egg over pan
  3. Seaon with S&P
  4. When the white is mostly cooked, flip egg for a few seconds
  5. Take out of pan and enjoy
