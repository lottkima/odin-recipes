Lasagna

Grandma's Gooey Lasagna

This is one of my favorite recipes of all time. A real comfort food for myself and my family.

Whenever I make this dish, it's sure to be a crowd fave.

    Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Ground Beef

    Directions

  1. Bring noodles to a boil
  2. Add sauce to beef
  3. Layer noodles, sauce and ricotta cheese
  4. Bake at 350 for an hour
